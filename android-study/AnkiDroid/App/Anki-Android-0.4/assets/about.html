AnkiDroid:
Flashcards for Android

Ankidroid can read .anki files produced by the desktop Anki software.

To help us make AnkiDroid better, please report any bug here, new feature ideas are very welcome too! Please use the wiki and say Hi on the forum.

Ankidroid is open source software, so everyone is encouraged to develop it :-) Non-developers can also help, for instance by updating the wiki and screenshots, donating to Anki, or blogging about AnkiDroid!
